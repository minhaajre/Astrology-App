# 3 DAYS

Creativity, Visibility, Communication

## 3rd of any month

Theme
Expression, content, social energy, charm, communication

Best for

* Posting content (LinkedIn, YouTube, ads)
* Sales outreach and warm networking
* Branding, design, creative work
* Public speaking, interviews, podcasts
* Announcements that need attention

Watch-outs

* Overpromising
* Gossip, sloppy wording
* Distraction and procrastination

Theme
Relationships, family, care, harmony, responsibility
