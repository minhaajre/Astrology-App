# NUMEROLOGY

(Dates)

Place this as the header of the Numerology card.

---

## Numerology — What It Means

**Numerology measures how a person or entity behaves over time.**

It shows:

* Life direction
* Rhythm and pace
* Day-to-day compatibility
* Long-term sustainability

---

## How Numerology Is Calculated

* All digits of the full date are added left-to-right
* The total is reduced to a single digit
* Master numbers 11, 22, 33 are preserved if applicable

Example format shown in UI:
03/08/1985 → 34 → 7

---

## How to Read Numerology Compatibility

* Same or compatible numbers → smoother life flow together
* Conflicting numbers → friction in priorities, pace, or routines

Numerology explains **how easy or difficult daily interaction feels**.

## Gematria (Latin) — What It Means

**Gematria (Latin) measures the public identity of a name.**

It reflects:

* How a name is perceived
* Communication style
* Branding and social compatibility
* Surface-level chemistry

This layer explains **first impressions and interaction tone**.

---

## How Gematria (Latin) Is Calculated

* Each Latin letter is converted using Pythagorean mapping
* Letter values are summed and reduced

Example shown visually:
M(4) I(9) N(5) H(8) A(1) A(1) J(1) → Total → Reduced

---

## How to Read Gematria (Latin) Compatibility

* Strong here → easy communication and social flow
* Weak here → misunderstandings or effort required

This layer affects **how things feel**, not necessarily how long they last.

## Gematria (Arabic / Abjad) — What It Means

**Abjad measures the structural and ontological weight of a name.**

This is the **deepest layer** in the system.

It governs:

* Intensity of connection
* Depth of resonance
* Why something feels “heavy,” “important,” or unavoidable
* Structural or karmic pressure

---

## How Abjad Is Calculated

* Arabic letters are normalized (diacritics removed, forms unified)
* Each letter is mapped using classical Abjad values (1–1000)
* The **full number is primary**
* A reduced value is shown only for comparison

UI display rule:

* Large number = full Abjad value
* Small badge = reduced value

---

## How to Read Abjad Compatibility

* High values = high intensity (not good or bad)
* Mismatch here = deep adaptation required
* Alignment here = powerful, stable resonance

This layer explains **why some connections feel profound but demanding**.
